Get Involved with Girls Who Code @ UTEP

Join Our Mission

Be part of a supportive community of women in tech who are breaking barriers and building the future. Whether you are new to coding or already experienced, there is a place for you here.

Ways to Get Involved:

  • Become a Member Attend weekly meetings, workshops, and coding sessions.
  • Collaborate on Projects Work with fellow students on exciting, real-world tech projects.
  • Learn & Grow Gain hands-on experience, mentorship, and career development opportunities.
  • Attend Events Participate in speaker panels, hackathons, and networking events.
  • Leadership Opportunities Step up as a future officer or event organizer.

Why Join?

By getting involved, you will not only sharpen your technical skills but also gain confidence, make friends, and join a nationwide movement to close the gender gap in technology.
