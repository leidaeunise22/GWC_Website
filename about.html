About Us

We Are Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code College Loops is a college community for women and non-binary students interested in tech and computer science that support one another in completing their degrees.

What makes Girls Who Code special is that we believe that in order to change the world you need more than just your coding skills. So, along with building strong ties and developing a supportive network of peers, we want you to see yourselves as: Leaders, Allies, and Learners.

College Loops members gain access to exclusive and/or early career readiness opportunities, such as meeting with industry professionals & corporate partners or participating in Girls Who Code workforce programs.
